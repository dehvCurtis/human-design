# Human Design Info

Provide information about Human Design concepts used in this app.

## Instructions

When the user asks about: `$ARGUMENTS`

Reference the Human Design constants in `lib/core/constants/human_design_constants.dart` and provide detailed explanations.

### Topics to cover:

**Gates (1-64)**
- Gate number, name, and keynote
- Which center it belongs to
- Connected channels
- Meaning and themes

**Centers (9)**
- Head: Inspiration, pressure to answer questions
- Ajna: Conceptualization, mental awareness
- Throat: Manifestation, communication
- G/Self: Identity, love, direction
- Heart/Ego: Willpower, ego, material world
- Sacral: Life force, sexuality, work capacity
- Solar Plexus: Emotions, feelings, awareness
- Spleen: Intuition, health, survival instincts
- Root: Adrenaline, stress, drive

**Types (5)**
- Manifestor: Initiator, informing strategy
- Generator: Builder, response strategy
- Manifesting Generator: Multi-tasker, response + inform
- Projector: Guide, invitation strategy
- Reflector: Mirror, lunar cycle strategy

**Authorities (7)**
- Emotional: Wait for clarity through emotional wave
- Sacral: Gut response (uh-huh/uh-uh)
- Splenic: Instant intuitive knowing
- Ego: What do I want? Will-based
- Self-Projected: Talking it out
- Environment: External cues
- Lunar: Wait a moon cycle (Reflectors only)

**Profiles (12)**
1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5, 3/5, 3/6, 4/6, 4/1, 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, 6/3
- Conscious line (personality)
- Unconscious line (design)

Example usage:
- `/hd-info gate 34` - Info about Gate 34
- `/hd-info sacral center` - Info about Sacral Center
- `/hd-info manifesting generator` - Info about MG type
- `/hd-info 3/5 profile` - Info about 3/5 Profile
